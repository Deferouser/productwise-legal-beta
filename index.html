BETA TESTER AGREEMENT

This Beta Tester Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between Defero Limited (“Company,” “we,” or “us”) and the individual testing the app Product Wise (“Tester” or “you”). By participating in the beta testing program, you agree to the terms below.

1. Tester Obligations

You agree to:

  • Test and use the App in good faith.
  • Provide feedback, error reports, and price data as requested.
  • Not copy, share, disclose, or distribute the App, screenshots, functions, or related materials without our prior written consent.
  • Refrain from reverse-engineering, modifying, or otherwise tampering with the App.
2. Confidentiality

The App, features, data, and related materials are confidential information. You agree not to disclose or share any part of it with others, including through social media, publications, or personal blogs, without our written permission.

3. Compensation & Expenses

  • You agree that your use of the App during this beta test is at your own risk.

  • To the fullest extent permitted by law, Defero Limited shall not be liable for any damages arising from the use of the App.
7. Terms & Termination
